### Ingredients

- 2 lbs boneless, skinless chicken breast
- 2 cups brown rice (uncooked)
- 2 cans black beans, drained and rinsed
- 2 bell peppers, diced
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt, pepper, garlic powder, and cumin to taste

### Instructions

1. Season chicken with salt, pepper, and garlic powder. Bake at 375°F for 25-30 minutes until cooked through. Let cool and slice.

2. Cook rice according to package directions.

3. Sauté onions and peppers in olive oil until softened. Season with cumin, salt, and pepper.

4. Divide into 6 meal prep containers: rice on bottom, then beans, sautéed vegetables, and sliced chicken on top.
